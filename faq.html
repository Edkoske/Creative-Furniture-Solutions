Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about custom orders, delivery, installation, care, and project timelines.

Do you build custom furniture?

Yes. We design and produce bespoke pieces for homes, offices, hospitality spaces, schools, restaurants, and commercial projects.

How long does a custom order take?

Most custom pieces take 3 to 8 weeks depending on scale, materials, finish, and installation requirements.

Do you offer delivery and installation?

Yes. We coordinate professional delivery and installation so each piece is placed, checked, and ready to use.

Can you furnish an entire room or office?

Yes. Our team can supply individual pieces or plan a complete furniture package for a full interior.
